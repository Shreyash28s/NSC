Personal Dashboard

Manage your dashboard to suit your needs!

User Authentication and Registration

Users should be able to create an account and log in securely. The registration process should collect necessary information, such as username, email, and password.

Customizable Layout

Allow users to customize the layout of their dashboard by rearranging widgets or components according to their preferences. Provide options to add, remove, or resize widgets as desired.

Profile Management

Users should have access to their profile settings where they can update personal information, profile picture, and preferences. Include options to manage privacy settings and notification preferences.

Widgets and Modules

Offer a variety of widgets or modules that users can add to their dashboard. Common widgets might include:

  • Calendar: Display upcoming events, appointments, or deadlines.
  • To-Do List: Manage tasks and set priorities.
  • News Feed: Display personalized news articles or updates.
  • Social Media Feeds: Integrate with social media platforms to view updates or notifications.

Encourage users to make the most out of their dashboard!
