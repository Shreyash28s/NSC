Membership Information

Eligibility

Membership is open to youth aged 13-25 who are passionate about making a positive impact in their community.

Benefits of Membership

  • Access to exclusive events and workshops
  • Networking opportunities with like-minded individuals
  • Opportunity to develop leadership and teamwork skills
  • Chance to contribute to meaningful community projects

How to Join

  1. Fill out the online membership form
  2. Attend a new member orientation session
  3. Pay the membership fee online or in person
  4. Start enjoying the benefits of membership!

Membership Fees

Membership fee: $25/year

Payment methods: Credit card, PayPal, or cash

Member Responsibilities

  • Regularly attend meetings and events
  • Participate in volunteer activities
  • Adhere to the organization's code of conduct
  • Contribute positively to the organization's mission
