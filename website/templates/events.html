Educational Reform & New Mental Health Policy

Following the rapid suicide rate in Mauritius among the youth, the Minister of education alongside the National Students Council worked together on a new mental health policy to better address the problems face by students without our school system.

After weeks of deliberation and planning, the full day workshop was orchestrated with 5 psychologists & executives of the Ministry in order to assess the upcoming measures that will be implemented.
